Startup Funding Options

1. Bootstrapping

Funding your business with personal savings and revenue:

  • Pros: Full control, no debt
  • Cons: Limited resources, slower growth

2. Angel Investors

Wealthy individuals who provide capital for startups:

  • Typical investment: $25k - $100k
  • Often take equity in return

3. Venture Capital

Professional investors funding high-growth startups:

  • Investments from $500k to millions
  • Require significant equity and control

4. Crowdfunding

Raising small amounts from many people online:

  • Platforms: Kickstarter, Indiegogo, GoFundMe
  • Reward-based or equity-based models

5. Small Business Loans

Traditional financing from banks or SBA:

  • Need good credit and business plan
  • Debt must be repaid with interest
